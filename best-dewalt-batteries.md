## Final Take
If you want the absolute safest bet, stick with **DeWalt OEM**. You’re paying extra for peace of mind and guaranteed Samsung cells.  

But if you’re not running tools 8 hours a day, the **Vanon replacements** are a sweet middle ground — they’ve proven themselves in real-world use.  

And for pure bang-for-buck, the **generic 4-pack** is hard to ignore. Just don’t expect OEM-level QC — think of them as great backups or budget-friendly workhorses.  

Bottom line: **OEM for pros, off-brand for savings.** A hybrid setup (one OEM + a couple knockoffs) might be the smartest move.
